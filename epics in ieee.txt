1. Project Title (Concise & Impactful)
Sustainable Urban Infrastructure: AI-Powered Energy Optimization and Predictive Maintenance of Municipal Tunnel Ventilation Systems
(Under 100 characters as required by pg 13 (p. 13))

2. Problem Statement & Justification
The Community Gap: Municipal tunnel ventilation fans (TVFs) are critical for public safety (smoke extraction) 
but are the city's largest energy consumers. Current maintenance is reactive, meaning fans are only fixed after they fail, risking commuter lives during fire emergencies. Additionally, they run at 100% speed 24/7, wasting massive taxpayer funds (p. 16).
Target End-Users: City Transit Authorities, tunnel maintenance crews, and everyday commuters (p. 16).

3. Community Partnership (NPO/NGO)
Partner: (Insert Local Municipality or Metro Rail Authority name).

4. Engineering Design & Technical Solution
Following the Human-Centered Design (HCD) phases (pg 7 & 16):
Requirements: Real-time monitoring of vibration, current, and airflow (p. 7).
Technical Approach:
Digital Twin: Developing a high-fidelity Simulink model to simulate "Normal" vs "Faulty" states (p. 16).
AI Engine: Implementing Isolation Forest for unsupervised anomaly detection.
XAI Layer: Using Explainable AI to provide "Maintenance Justification Reports" for non-technical municipal workers (p. 10).
Innovation: Fusing energy optimization with predictive safety in a single AI framework (p. 10).
